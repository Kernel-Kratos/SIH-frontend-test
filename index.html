About JAN-AWAZ

JAN-AWAZ, meaning "The People's Voice" in Hindi, is a non-profit initiative born from a simple idea: that every citizen has the right to a clean, safe, and functional community. We believe that technology can be a powerful bridge between citizens and governance.

Our mission is to empower individuals to become active participants in improving their neighborhoods. By providing a simple, accessible platform to report local civic issues—from potholes and broken streetlights to water leaks and garbage collection problems—we aim to bring these problems to the attention of the authorities who can solve them.

Every report you submit is a step towards a more accountable and responsive system. Together, we can build stronger communities, one report at a time. Thank you for being a part of this movement.
